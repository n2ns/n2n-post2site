# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want N2N Post2Site to be a practical, respectful, and welcoming open-source project for AI developer tooling.

Participants are expected to:

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent while staying clear and direct.
- Keep discussions focused on the project.
- Avoid harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or restrict participation when behavior makes the project less safe or productive for others.

To report a concern, contact the maintainers through the repository owner or organization contact channels.
